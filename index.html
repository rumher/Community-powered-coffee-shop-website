Our Roots

More Than Just Coffee

Block & Bean started in 2014 when two neighbors — a former barista and a local baker — decided their block needed a gathering place. Not just another cafe, but a living room for the neighborhood.

Today, we're still family-owned, still sourcing from local roasters, and still believing that the best conversations happen over a really good cup of coffee. Our walls are covered in photos of regulars because you are what makes this place special.

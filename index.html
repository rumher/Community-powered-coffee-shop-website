Our Roots

Born on Birch Street

We started Block & Bean in 2014 with a simple mission: create a space where coffee is serious, but people are welcome just as they are. No pretension, just great beans and good vibes. We source directly from sustainable farms and bake our pastries fresh every morning at 4 AM.

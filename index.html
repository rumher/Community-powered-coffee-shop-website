Our Roots

More Than Just Caffeine

Block & Bean started in 2014 with a simple mission: to build a gathering spot that feels like an extension of your own home. We source beans directly from sustainable farms and roast them locally to bring out the unique character of every origin.

Whether you're here for a quick espresso at the bar or a long afternoon working on your novel, you're part of the block now.
